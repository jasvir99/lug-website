LUG

A Linux User Group(LUG) is a private, generally non-profit organization that provides support and/or education for Linux users, particularly for inexperienced users.
LUG Ludhiana promotes the use of Free And Open Source Software(FOSS) among students to avoid piracy crimes besides encouraging the use of Linux as their primary operating system for day to day tasks.
